Remnants is a unique, innovative mod that rebuilds your Terraria world from the ground up, enriching it with new environments to explore and secrets to discover.

In summary, this mod adds:
 . A complete overhaul to ALL vanilla world generation
 . An assortment of new content, including a massive expansion to the aether biome
 . Minor gameplay improvements, most notably reworks to some enemy AI

See the steam workshop page for more details.
